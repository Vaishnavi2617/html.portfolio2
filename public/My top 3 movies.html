The Best Movies According to Vaishnavi

My top 3 movies of all-time

Wake Up Sid

This is my favourite movie because I could resonate with the lead character of the movie. I love the story line.

Tara Rum Pum

This has to be my 2nd favourite movie as this movie has a great story line, a great lesson and one of favorite bollywood reel pair as the lead characters.

Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

This has to be my 3rd favourite movie as this movie has my favourite actor and teaches you that always chase your dreams but not on the cost of losing the touch with your old friends and family.
